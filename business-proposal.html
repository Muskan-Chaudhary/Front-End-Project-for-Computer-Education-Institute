NICT’s RESPONSIBILITIES

1. The Course Content, the training material for imparting the courses will be provided by NICT
2. The Institute/Engineer/Faculty training will be provided by NICT
3. Up-gradation of the courses to keep abreast with latest technologies will NICT’s responsibility
4. NICT will share the expertise and experience in assisting the Franchise to promote the courses. NICT will also guide the franchise in the right approach for marketing and advertising from time to time
5. Upon successful completion of the course NICT will provide certificate from their Head Office

SOLUTION OFFERED THROUGH THE FRANCHISE

1. NICT has strategic partnership with several major worldwide companies and has built expertise in the area of network security
2. NICT will provide total technical training and support to the franchise/Partner in selling /promoting the above solution
3. The entire gamut of its Networking/Security products and solutions will be offered through this partnership adding traditional revenue stream to the franchise security like Firewall, IBS, IPS, and content and interest security products
4. NICT will provide website support and enquiries/lead sharing generated out of NICT’s website and other marketing sources/contacts.

Franchise Responsibilities:-

1. Total investment like Server Router switches, Computers, Furniture etc. and other equipment will be the franchise responsibility.
2. The marketing/selling of the courses in its territory will be the franchise responsibility.
3. Setting up the office, staff to manage and running the complete operations will be the Franchise responsibility.
4. The Advertisement/promotion in the local media will be the franchise’s responsibility.
5. The franchise will appoint the Engineers/Faculty at their cost and send them to NICT (Hyderabad) for training and NICT will provide the necessary training at free of cost.
